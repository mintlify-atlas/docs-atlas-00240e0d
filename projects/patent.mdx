## Patent Overview

**United States Patent #9,276,418** covers an innovative battery exchange kiosk system with integrated remote telemetry and automated profit & loss tracking. Granted by the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO), this utility patent protects the intellectual property behind the Green Box Batteries commercial product.
